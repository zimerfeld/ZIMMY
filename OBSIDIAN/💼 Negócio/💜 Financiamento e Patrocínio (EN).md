# 💜 Funding and Sponsorship

Since Zimmy Pet is **free and open source** (no direct sale), monetization is **indirect**:
donations/sponsorship and portfolio value. Growth strategy in
[[🚀 Distribuição e Crescimento (EN)|Distribution and Growth]]; metrics in
[[📈 Adoção e Métricas (EN)|Adoption & Metrics]].

## 💰 Support channels
- **GitHub Sponsors:** `zimerfeld` — **Sponsor** button on the repository
  https://github.com/zimerfeld/ZIMMY.
- **Ko-fi:** `C0D621FCGD` — one-off donation link (https://ko-fi.com/C0D621FCGD).
- **Star ⭐:** the cheapest, highest-reach CTA — ask for a ⭐ on the repo in every promo
  copy ([[📣 Divulgação — Posts (EN)|Promo Posts]]).

- **Promo posts:** fixed CTA "⭐ on the repo + 💜 Sponsor (GitHub Sponsors `zimerfeld` /
  Ko-fi `C0D621FCGD`)" — see [[📣 Divulgação — Posts (EN)|Promo Posts]].

## 🏷️ Badges (social proof)
At the top of the README, dynamic **stars** and **adoption** badges reinforce traction
before asking for support:
- ⭐ **Stars:** `https://img.shields.io/github/stars/zimerfeld/ZIMMY`
- 📈 **Adoption/clones:** the project's live metric is **clones** (14-day window); the repos
  don't publish release assets, so *downloads = 0*. See [[📈 Adoção e Métricas (EN)|Adoption & Metrics]]
  for the counting procedure (`gh api .../traffic/clones`).

## 📈 Investment thesis
Direct financial support tends to be modest for a free pet; the **real return** is
**portfolio + top of funnel** (the pet sits on screen all day, showcasing the other
Zimerfeld products) and **social proof** (stars/clones) that sustains the brand. Low
marginal cost (a single binary, no server) keeps financial risk near zero.
